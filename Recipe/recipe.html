Description

This classic Margherita pizza features a crisp crust, tangy tomato sauce, creamy mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil leaves. It's a simple yet delicious recipe that brings the taste of Italy to your kitchen.

Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 12 minutes | Yield: 1 large pizza


Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano (optional)


Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
    4. ..........
  4. Remove from oven and top with fresh basil leaves.
  5. Let cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
  6. Enjoy your homemade Margherita pizza fresh and hot.


Tips

  • For a crispier crust, bake the pizza on a preheated pizza stone.
  • Use high-quality mozzarella and fresh basil for the best flavor.
  • Let the dough rest at room temperature before rolling for easier handling.
  • Add a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil before serving for extra richness.

Recipe inspired by the classic Margherita pizza from Naples, Italy

The Margherita pizza was named in honor of Queen Margherita of Savoy in 1889
